Tour Plan

This itinerary carefully balances high-altitude travel with cultural exploration, featuring a dedicated arrival day for acclimatization and a journey through the scenic Indus Valley.

Day 1: Arrival in Leh – Acclimatization & Leisure

Arrive at Leh Airport and transfer to your hotel. The day is reserved for rest and acclimatization to help your body adjust to the high altitude. In the evening, you may take a gentle walk through Leh Market or visit the serene Shanti Stupa, offering panoramic views of Leh town and the surrounding mountains. Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 2: Leh Local Sightseeing Tour

After breakfast, proceed for a comprehensive Leh local sightseeing tour. Visit the majestic Thiksey Monastery, one of the largest monasteries in Ladakh, followed by the renowned Hemis Monastery, famous for its ancient artifacts and spiritual significance. Explore the historic Leh Palace and the informative Hall of Fame Museum, which showcases Ladakh’s culture and the Indian Army’s legacy. The evening is free to explore the lively Leh Bazaar. Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 3: Leh to Alchi – Buddhist Heritage & Indus Valley Tour

Drive through the scenic Indus Valley to reach Alchi, home to the oldest monastery in Ladakh. Visit the Alchi Monastery, renowned for its exquisite 11th-century murals and wooden sculptures. Continue to the historic Basgo Palace ruins, a former royal residence offering spectacular views of the Indus River. Overnight stay in Alchi.

Day 4: Alchi to Pangong Lake – Scenic Himalayan Drive

After breakfast, embark on a breathtaking drive towards the iconic Pangong Lake, one of the most beautiful high-altitude lakes in the world. En route, stop at Chumathang Hot Springs, known for their natural therapeutic properties. Reach Pangong by evening and enjoy leisure time along the tranquil lakeshore, famous for its changing shades of blue. Overnight stay near Pangong Lake.

Day 5: Pangong Lake to Nubra Valley via Khardung La

Wake up to stunning views of Pangong Lake before driving towards Nubra Valley, crossing the legendary Khardung La Pass, one of the highest motorable roads in the world. Upon arrival in Nubra, visit the hilltop Diskit Monastery and explore the unique Hunder Sand Dunes, where you can enjoy a camel safari on double-humped Bactrian camels. Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

Day 6: Nubra Valley – Turtuk Village or Siachen Base Camp Excursion

Today, choose between an excursion to Turtuk Village, one of the northernmost villages in India known for its unique culture and scenic beauty, or a visit towards the Siachen Base Camp (subject to permits and accessibility). Experience the raw landscapes, remote villages, and traditional Ladakhi lifestyle. Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

Day 7: Nubra Valley to Leh – Return Journey

After breakfast, drive back to Leh, enjoying scenic halts along riversides and mountain viewpoints. The evening is free for last-minute shopping, café hopping, or simply relaxing at your hotel. Overnight stay in Leh.

Day 8: Departure from Leh

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Leh Airport for your onward journey, concluding your unforgettable Ladakh adventure tour.
