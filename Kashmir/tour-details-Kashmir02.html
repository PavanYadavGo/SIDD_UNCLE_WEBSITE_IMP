About your holidays

Discover the breathtaking beauty of the Kashmir Valley with the 6 Nights / 7 Days Majestic Kashmir Explorer. This journey takes you through the heart of the Himalayas, offering a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and serenity. From the iconic houseboats of Srinagar to the snow sports of Gulmarg and the glaciers of Sonmarg, this tour is designed for those seeking a deep immersion into nature's wonders.

Tour Plan

This itinerary focuses on iconic destinations including the Aru Valley, the Gondola cable car in Gulmarg, and the glaciers of Sonmarg.

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar – Houseboat Stay

Arrive and check into your Dal Lake houseboat. Experience the calm waters, enjoy a welcome drink, and relax while taking in the scenic lake views.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Visit Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh, followed by a walk through Lal Chowk. In the evening, enjoy a tranquil Shikara ride, capturing the reflections of snow-capped mountains.

Day 3: Srinagar Exploration & Handicraft Shopping

Explore Shankaracharya Temple and indulge in shopping for Pashmina shawls, handcrafted jewelry, and Kashmiri carpets. Evening free for leisure.

Day 4: Pahalgam – Adventure & Scenic Views

Drive to Pahalgam, visit Aru Valley, enjoy river walks or pony rides. Relax amidst lush pine forests and the gurgling Lidder River. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 5: Gulmarg – Snow Sports & Gondola Ride

Drive to Gulmarg, take the Gondola cable car for panoramic views, explore meadows, or engage in snow activities. Capture photography of the breathtaking landscapes. Overnight in Gulmarg.

Day 6: Sonmarg Day Trip

Drive to Sonmarg for its glacier views, short hikes, and photography. Return to Srinagar in the evening and enjoy a relaxed dinner.

Day 7: Departure

Drive to Srinagar airport/railway station for departure.
