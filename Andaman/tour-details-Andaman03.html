Andaman Beyond Beaches – Baratang & Havelock Explorer

Port Blair • Baratang Island • Havelock Island

Durations5 Nights 06 Days

About your holiday

Go beyond the typical shoreline with the "Andaman Beyond Beaches" explorer. This 5 Nights / 6 Days journey is designed for travelers who crave unique geological wonders and raw tropical wilderness. Venture through the Jarawa Tribal Reserve to witness the naturally formed Limestone Caves and the rare Mud Volcanoes of Baratang, before heading to the pristine, world-class beaches of Havelock. It’s the perfect balance of rugged exploration and island relaxation.

Tour Plan

A unique itinerary that combines the historical landmarks of Port Blair with the dense forests of North & Middle Andaman and the turquoise waters of the Ritchie's Archipelago.

Arrival in Port Blair & Cellular Jail

Arrive at Port Blair Airport and transfer to your hotel. In the evening, visit the historic Cellular Jail, a landmark that reflects the rich history and freedom struggle of the Andaman Islands. Spend the evening exploring the surroundings or relaxing at leisure. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Baratang Island: Limestone Caves & Mud Volcano

Start early for a scenic drive through the Jarawa Tribal Reserve to Baratang Island, one of the most unique destinations in the Andamans. Visit the fascinating Limestone Caves, formed naturally over thousands of years, and witness the rare Mud Volcano, a geological wonder. Return to Port Blair by evening. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Transfer to Havelock & Radhanagar Beach

After breakfast, board a ferry to Havelock Island, renowned for its pristine beaches and turquoise waters. Upon arrival, unwind at the world-famous Radhanagar Beach, known for its soft white sands and breathtaking views—perfect for relaxation and photography. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

Elephant Beach & Water Activities

After breakfast, proceed to Elephant Beach, a popular spot for snorkeling, coral viewing, and water activities. The shallow reefs and vibrant marine life make it ideal for both beginners and adventure enthusiasts. The rest of the day is free to enjoy the island at your own pace. Overnight stay in Havelock Island.

Return to Port Blair & Sunset at Chidiya Tapu

Return to Port Blair by ferry in the morning. Later, visit ChidiyaTapu, famous for its scenic landscapes and mesmerizing sunset views—an ideal way to end your island exploration. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Farewell & Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and enjoy a comfortable airport transfer, concluding your unforgettable Andaman Islands tour.
